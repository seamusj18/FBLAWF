Operation Warm Fuzzy is a non-profit organization that was started as a class project at


Millikin University. The group realized that hospitals, police, fire, and other similar groups see people at some


of their worst times. The founders of the organization wanted to make those times a little easier


by supplying teddy bears (a warm fuzzy) and other items to families and children in need.
